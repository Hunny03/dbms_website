Question 1: Display employee name, hire date, and current date. Also calculate the number of days the employee has worked.

Show employee names, their hire dates, current system date, and calculate tenure in days.

SQL Command:
SELECT ENAME, HIREDATE, SYSDATE, SYSDATE - HIREDATE AS DAYS_WORKED FROM EMP;
Output:
ENAME HIREDATE SYSDATE DAYS_WORKED
SMITH 17-DEC-80 05-APR-26 16545

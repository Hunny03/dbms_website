Question 1: Display employee name and department name.

Show employee names along with their department names using INNER JOIN.

Question 21: Display department with maximum employees.

Find the department with the highest number of employees.

SQL Command:
SELECT D.DNAME, COUNT(*) AS EMP_COUNT FROM DEPT D JOIN EMP E ON D.DEPTNO = E.DEPTNO GROUP BY D.DNAME, D.DEPTNO HAVING COUNT(*) = (SELECT MAX(COUNT(*)) FROM EMP GROUP BY DEPTNO);
Output:
DNAME EMP_COUNT
RESEARCH 5
Question 22: Display department with highest total salary.

Find the department with the highest total salary expenditure.

SQL Command:
SELECT D.DNAME, SUM(E.SAL) AS TOTAL_SALARY FROM DEPT D JOIN EMP E ON D.DEPTNO = E.DEPTNO GROUP BY D.DNAME, D.DEPTNO HAVING SUM(E.SAL) = (SELECT MAX(SUM(SAL)) FROM EMP GROUP BY DEPTNO);
Output:
DNAME TOTAL_SALARY
RESEARCH 10875
